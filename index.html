School Companion – Legal Pages

This repository contains the public legal pages required for the School Companion mobile application. These pages are hosted through GitHub Pages and linked in the Google Play Store listing.

Included Pages

1. Privacy Policy

Location: index.html
Describes how School Companion collects, stores and uses personal data.

2. Account Deletion Instructions

Location: delete.html
Explains how users can request deletion of their account and all associated data.

Purpose

Google Play requires app developers to provide public URLs for:

  • Privacy Policy
  • Account and data deletion instructions

This repository provides those pages in a simple and accessible format.
