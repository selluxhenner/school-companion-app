Account Deletion – School Companion Last updated: 30.11.2025 If you want to delete your School Companion account and all associated data, please follow the steps below. How to request deletion Send an email to: p.henner.18@gmail.com Subject: Account deletion – School Companion Include the email address of the account you want to delete. What will be deleted When your account is deleted, we permanently remove: Your user account (Firebase Authentication) Timetable data Exams, homework, deadlines Pomodoro sessions Friends / group data connected to your account Notification tokens Profile information (name, avatar, class) Processing time We usually delete all data within 7 days. Retention Some technical logs (e.g., security logs) may be kept for up to 30 days for fraud prevention and safety purposes. They are not linked to your identity. If you have questions, contact: info@serviweb.ch
