Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy – School Companion Last updated: 30.11.2025 School Companion (“the App”) is a study and timetable helper for students at Kantonsschulen and similar schools. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use and protect your data. 1. Data we collect When you use School Companion, we may collect: Account data Email address Password (stored only as a secure hash) Optional profile information (name, avatar, school, class) App content you enter Timetable and lessons Exams, homework, deadlines and reminders Pomodoro sessions and study statistics Optional friends / study groups (friend codes, basic profile info) Device & usage data Device type, operating system, app version Crash reports and basic analytics (how often features are used) Notification tokens so that we can send reminders We do not collect any financial data or exact location (GPS) and we do not show third-party advertising. 2. How we use your data We use your data to: Provide the core functions of the App (timetable, exams, reminders, Pomodoro, friends / groups) Sync your data between your devices via the cloud Send notifications that you have enabled (exam reminders, study reminders etc.) Improve the App, fix bugs and understand which features are used Keep the service secure (for example, prevent abuse and protect accounts) We do not sell your data and we do not share it with third parties for marketing. 3. Legal basis (EU / Switzerland) If you are located in the EU or Switzerland, we process your data based on: Contract – to provide you with the App and its functions Legitimate interest – to keep the service secure and improve it Consent – for optional features like push notifications or analytics, where required You can withdraw your consent at any time in the app settings or on your device. 4. Third-party services School Companion uses the following providers to run the app: Google Firebase (Firebase Authentication, Firestore / Realtime Database, Firebase Storage, Cloud Functions, Optional Crashlytics / Analytics) Provider: Google LLC and its affiliates. Data is stored on Google servers which may be located in the EU or other countries. Google acts as our data processor and is bound by data protection agreements. We only share the minimum data required for these services to work. 5. Data storage and security Your data is stored securely in Firebase. We use authentication, access control and encryption provided by Firebase and by the device (for example, HTTPS). We keep data only as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law. 6. Children’s privacy School Companion is designed for students from about 13 years and older (secondary school level). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe that a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us so we can delete it. 7. Your rights Depending on your country, you may have the right to: Access the personal data we store about you Correct incorrect data Delete your account and data (“right to be forgotten”) Restrict or object to certain processing Receive a copy of your data in a portable format You can delete most data directly inside the app (for example exams, timetable, account). If you want to request access or deletion of all data, contact us using the details below. 8. Data retention If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymise your personal data within a reasonable time, except where we must keep it for legal reasons (for example, security logs). 9.
